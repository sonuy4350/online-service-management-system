About SmartFix Service Centre

SmartFix Service Centre is a trusted and customer-focused home appliance and electronics repair company established in 2022. We specialize in delivering fast, reliable, and affordable repair services for households and small businesses.

Our goal is to simplify the service management process by providing a transparent system where customers can raise service requests, track progress, and receive digital receipts — all through a centralized platform.

To provide high-quality repair services with honesty, professionalism, and technical excellence while ensuring customer satisfaction.

👁 Our Vision

To become the most reliable and technology-driven service management company in India by integrating smart systems and automation.
